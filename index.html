Who We Are

Devarsons is an ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001 accredited company, developing and producing specialty colorants. The group was established in 1923 with manufacturing activity primarily based in Ahmedabad. With over 50 years of accumulated experience in Color Application, our product range is specifically designed to cater to customers in various industries such as Cosmetics, Crop Protection, Plastics, Petroleum, etc.

The emphasis within the organization is on developing new molecules or customizing current known molecules to meet highly specialized applications. Devarsons does not produce any one class of dye but rather an assortment of dyes with different chemistries to suit highly evolved applications. Our dyes comply with various worldwide regulations and our plant and product packaging are designed to eliminate foreign contamination.

Milestones

102 Years of Innovation and Excellence

1923

Foundation

Devarsons Group established as a trading company. Focus since inception was on being a "solution provider" rather than a dye supplier.

1960-70

Manufacturing Begins

Manufacturing established with Solvent Yellow 14 and Solvent Red 24. Targeted industries were Cosmetic and Petroleum.

1976

Odhav Facility

Established Manufacturing facility in Odhav, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, INDIA

1978

First Export

First Export of Solvent Red 24 in container load to ICI Australia

1990

Global Partnership

ICI UK (later Zeneca, Avecia) decided to source solvent dyes from Devarsons. Solvent dye range expanded. Liquid Solvent Dyes, Direct Dyes introduced.

1992

Innovation Milestone

Successfully developed Kerosene Marker Dye for Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL). First time launch "Blue Liquid Dye" for kerosene in India.

2005

ISO Certification

Environmental, Health and Safety

Infrastructure

We aim to use natural resources efficiently and minimize the environmental impacts of our activities and products during their life cycles.

EHS Policy

At Devarsons, protection of environment, occupational health and safety of employees is a prime and important objective for sustainable development.

We are committed to:

  • Complying with relevant laws and regulations with additional measures considered necessary
  • Follow scientific approach to minimize waste generation thereby achieving cleaner production
  • Continual improvement in housekeeping
  • Making this policy available to the public
